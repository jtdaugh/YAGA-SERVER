Instant Video Messaging

Yaga isn't live video chat, its a permanent conversation. Every message is a video, and sending one is never more than a tap away. Create a group video chat for your roommates or family, or chat one on one with a friend. Once you've sent a video, others in the chat can like the video, comment right on top of your video, or reply with a video of their own. You can also add captions or wacky filters to your video messages. Oh, and your videos will never expire on Yaga. You can always choose to delete a video, but you'll never lose them.
